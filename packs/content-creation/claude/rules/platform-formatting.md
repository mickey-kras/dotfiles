# Platform Formatting

When producing content for a specific channel, respect that platform's conventions:

- LinkedIn: professional tone, 1300 character sweet spot for posts, line breaks for readability, 3-5 hashtags maximum
- Twitter/X: 280 character limit per tweet, thread structure for longer content, 1-2 hashtags
- Blog/article: proper heading hierarchy (H1 -> H2 -> H3), scannable paragraphs, internal and external links
- Email newsletter: subject line under 50 characters, preview text, single clear CTA
- Video script: spoken word pacing (~150 words per minute), visual cues, hook in first 5 seconds

Do not apply one platform's conventions to another. When the target platform is unspecified, ask before formatting.
