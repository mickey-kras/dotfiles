# Methodology Transparency

Every research deliverable must state how findings were produced:

- what sources were searched and why those sources were chosen
- what search terms, filters, or criteria were applied
- what time period the evidence covers
- what was excluded and why (scope limits, quality thresholds, access constraints)
- what analytical method was used (thematic analysis, statistical comparison, expert review)

If the methodology has known limitations (small sample, single geography, outdated sources), state them upfront rather than burying them in caveats.

Reproducibility matters: another researcher with the same inputs should reach similar conclusions.
