# Hypothesis Tracking

Use this skill to maintain a structured record of research hypotheses, their
supporting and contradicting evidence, and current confidence levels.

Format per hypothesis:
- hypothesis statement (falsifiable, specific)
- confidence level: high, medium, low, or unresolved
- supporting evidence with source references
- contradicting evidence with source references
- open questions that could shift confidence
- last updated date

Process:
1. state the hypothesis clearly before gathering evidence
2. log each piece of evidence as supporting, contradicting, or ambiguous
3. update confidence level after each evidence review
4. flag when a hypothesis should be split, merged, or retired
5. produce a hypothesis summary table for stakeholder review
